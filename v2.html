Our home

Not a kennel. A real, lived-in home.

We started Cat's Centara because boarding shouldn't feel clinical. Here, the day moves slowly: morning sun on the floorboards, an afternoon stretch, a cushion that's always warm. Your cat settles in like family.

  • Sunlit lounging — south-facing perches that catch the warm afternoon light.
  • Familiar routines — we keep your cat's feeding times and quirks just the way they like.
  • Always within earshot — soft music, gentle voices, never a stressful clatter.
Everyday comforts

The little things that feel like home

Every detail is shaped around how cats actually like to live — warm, unhurried, and entirely their own.

Snug bedding

Plush, freshly laundered blankets and tucked-away nooks for the deepest, most undisturbed naps.

Home-style menu

Gently prepared meals tailored to each guest, served on a schedule that matches life at home.

Gentle play

Feather wands, sunbeam chases, and quiet enrichment — at a pace each cat chooses for themselves.

Watchful care

Trained carers keep a quiet eye on every guest, with a vet on call should anyone need a little extra.

Daily photo notes

A warm little update each day — a snapshot and a few words so you can relax while you're away.

Calm by design

Soft lighting, soundproofed rooms, and unhurried days keep tails relaxed and whiskers content.
