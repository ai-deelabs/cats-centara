Our home

Not a kennel — a cozy little house full of warmth

Cat's Centara began with a simple belief: that cats deserve a place that feels like home, even when they're away from yours. No cold cages, no clinical halls — just soft corners, gentle light, and people who genuinely adore cats.

Every room is arranged for comfort and calm, with familiar textures, quiet spaces to hide, and plenty of sunlit spots to stretch out and dream.

— Mira & the Centara family
